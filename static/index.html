Wacky Watch

Wacky Watch is a simulator of those very watches from the series The Amazing Digital Circus. Upload any images and see them appear on the screen.

This website is a fork of the GreepTheSheep project with new features.

The original series The Amazing Digital Circus was created by Glitch Productions and artist Gooseworx. This website is not affiliated with them and is a non‑commercial fan project.
